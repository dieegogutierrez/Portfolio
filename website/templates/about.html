Who am I?

I am a Brazilian mechanical engineer based in Canada with a passion for transforming data into actionable insights. My career spans diverse industries, where I’ve consistently used data analytics to solve complex problems and drive measurable improvements.

At a bioenergy power plant, I developed a KPI dashboard using data from ERP systems and spreadsheets, reducing sugar processing downtime and speeding up maintenance decision-making. As the owner of a café and ice cream shop, I applied data analysis to sales trends, implementing targeted marketing campaigns that increased customer engagement and optimized inventory management to reduce waste. In the truck logistics industry, I built an end-to-end ETL pipeline and created dashboards to monitor revenue efficiency using Python, improving data accessibility and business insights.

Now, as a data freelancer, I collaborate with businesses across various sectors to uncover insights, build scalable data solutions, and empower decision-making. My goal is to help organizations harness the power of their data to achieve their strategic objectives.
